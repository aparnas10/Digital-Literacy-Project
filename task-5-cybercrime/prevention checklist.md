# Stay Safe from Online Payment Fraud

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone.
- Remember that to RECEIVE money, you do NOT need to enter a PIN. 
- Always check if it is a "Pay" or "Collect Request" before approving.  
- Do not click on unknown payment links.  
- Verify the identity of buyers/sellers before transactions. 
- Avoid responding to calls claiming to be from bank support.  
- Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.).  
- Enable app lock and security features on your phone.

To report any issues, contact: National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or Helpline no. 1930.
