For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva, an online design tool that allows users to create visually 
appealing graphics easily. The aim of the infographic was to educate students aboutthe importance of digital literacy in today’s world.
The infographic highlights key areas such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and maintaining a professional online
presence I included important points like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and using platformssuch as Google Docs, GitHub,
and LinkedIn for academicand professional growth. The content was presented in a structured and visually engaging format using icons, colors,
and minimal text to improve readability.This task helped me understand the importance of spreading digital awareness effectively through
visual communication.
